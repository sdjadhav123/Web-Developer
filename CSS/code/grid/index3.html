MAHARASTRA

Maharashtra is a state in western India with a rich and diverse culture, shaped by historical events, religious beliefs, and regional variations. It's known for its vibrant festivals, particularly Ganesh Chaturthi and Diwali, as well as traditional dance forms like Lavani. Marathi is the official language and Hinduism is the dominant religion, though other faiths also have a presence.
